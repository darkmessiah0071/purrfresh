## 1. Overview

PurrFresh Daily Cat Photos FREE ("the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension that displays unique cat photos fetched from The Cat API. This Privacy Policy explains what data the Extension collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

**Summary: We collect no personal data. Everything is stored locally on your device.**

---

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2a. Data stored locally on your device only

The Extension stores the following data exclusively in your browser's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`). This data **never leaves your device** and is **never transmitted to us**:

| Data | Purpose | Stored Where |
|------|---------|-------------|
| List of seen image IDs | Prevents showing the same cat photo twice | Your device only |
| Favorite image URLs | Saves your favorited cat photos | Your device only |
| Daily view count & date | Enforces the free tier daily limit | Your device only |
| TheCatAPI key (if entered) | Passed directly to The Cat API for higher rate limits | Your device only |
| UI preferences (dark mode, breed display) | Remembers your settings | Your device only |
| Total cats seen (count) | Displays lifetime stats | Your device only |

### 2b. Data we do NOT collect

- We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information.
- We do not collect browsing history or track which websites you visit.
- We do not use analytics, telemetry, or crash reporting services.
- We do not use advertising networks or sell any data.
- We do not transmit any data to our own servers (we have none).

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

The Extension communicates with two external services:

### The Cat API (`api.thecatapi.com`)
- **Purpose:** Fetches cat photos to display to you.
- **Data sent:** Your IP address (standard for any HTTP request), and your API key if you have entered one in Settings.
- **Their privacy policy:** https://thecatapi.com/privacy
- **What we receive:** Random cat image URLs and metadata (breed info). No personal data.

### ExtensionPay (`extensionpay.com`)
- **Purpose:** Processes payments for premium subscription tiers (Monthly, Yearly, Lifetime).
- **Data sent:** Only if you choose to subscribe. ExtensionPay handles payment processing; we never see your payment card details.
- **Their privacy policy:** https://extensionpay.com/privacy
- **What we receive:** A boolean `paid: true/false` and your chosen plan name, used only to unlock premium features.

---

## 4. Permissions Justification

The Extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

| Permission | Why it's needed |
|-----------|----------------|
| `storage` | Saves your seen list, favorites, and settings locally on your device |
| `alarms` | Resets the daily cat counter at midnight each day |
| `tabs` | Opens the ExtensionPay payment page and cat viewer in a new tab/window |
| `windows` | Opens the expandable cat viewer as a popup window |

We do not request permissions to read your browsing history, access website content, capture your screen, or access your clipboard beyond explicitly user-triggered copy actions.

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

The Extension is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

---

## 6. Data Retention & Deletion

All locally stored data can be deleted at any time:
- **Reset seen list:** Settings tab → "Reset Seen List"
- **Clear favorites:** Settings tab → "Clear Favorites"
- **Full removal:** Uninstalling the Extension from Chrome automatically removes all locally stored data.
